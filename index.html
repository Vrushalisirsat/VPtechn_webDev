Who we are



A non-profit organisation that enables senior citizens to lead active lives and provides trusted information, productive ageing opportunities and social support services.

What we do



We create an enlightened society in which senior citizens feel secure, confident, valued and can live with dignity. We rid fear from their minds and abolish their misconceptions.

Why we do it



We are guided by our optimistic view of the world and the power of positive thinking. We are also confident, relentless and tireless in our pursuits for elder care
